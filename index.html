Sunny

Boy

A Pups Life

By Susie Garcia

Hello" my name is Sunny Boy and I was born in Texas. After
three days I ended up traveling from Texas to Arizona. I was
on my way to meet my new family and other puppies living there.
Traveling from Texas to Arizona was a long drive but lots of
fun. Since I left my mother early I had to be bottle feed. I
enjoyed the ride and was glad when I finally go to my new
home. Now it was time to meet my new family.
I am very excited to meet my new family and I hope they love
me. My new family is so nice and lovable. I am so happy to be
here. Can't wait to start this new life here.
I am really enjoying my new family and home. I enjoy going
outside in the yard and watching my family have a great time
playing ball and laying in the sun.
Now that I am alittle bigger I go with kids in my family to the
park and watch them play. Bonita likes sitting on the slide
and Bosco likes laying in the grass enjoying the sun.
I am six months now and it is time for me to visit the doctor to
get my first checkup. My doctor is very nice and friendly. I am
scared because I have to get a three shots. I hope it does not hurt.
Today the groomer is coming to give all of us a bath and cut.
When we are done we get to have Military bandanas put on
all of us. I have never had a bath I wonder how it will feel.
Hopefully it will feel nice.
I am all nice and clean
with my shots up to date.
Now I can go out and play
with the other puppies and
have some fun.
When my mommy goes shopping to the Pet Store I always get
to go and pick out new toys. She puts me in the bags or her
purse so I can see everything as we walk the aisle. She lets
me pick whatever I want when we are there.
Bosco, Bonita and I always sit on the couch waiting for them
to get home. We always miss tme when they are gone and get
really excited when they all return home to us.
Sometimes when my mommy is working on her computer
I get to help. I like it when we call people on it and I can see
them. The first I saw myself on it I bark and growled. My family
laughed at me they said I "so cute."
Today I got in trouble
because I decided to tear
some papers out of a book.
I was put in timeout for
awhile and did not like that my
mommy being mad at me.
Bosco, Bonita and I are waiting for mommy and daddy
to come to bed. Every night we get to sleep in the big
with mommy and daddy. I love it because it is always
nice and warm.
The End
